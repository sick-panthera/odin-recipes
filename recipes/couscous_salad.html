Couscous Salad

Description

Just like the picture (but without the filthy olives).

Ingredients

  • 100g couscous
  • 200ml hot vegetable stock
  • 2 spring onions
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 50g feta cheese
  • 2 tbsp pesto
  • Diced tomato

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, pour the hot stock over the couscous. Cover and leave for 10 minutes until fluffy and all the stock has been absorbed.
  2. Look man, from here it's a salad. Cut it up and lets it get familiar.
  3. "Chow down my dude."
