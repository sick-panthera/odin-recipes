Crispy Sweet Chilli Beef

Description

Sticky, sweet and spicy.

Make alongside some rice and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 350g beef, thinly sliced
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 tsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch (enough to coat)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1 onion
  • 1 red/green chilli
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2 tbsp sriracha
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Add egg, salt, pepper and sugar to the beef. Add cornstarch to get even, sticky coatig.
  2. Over medium high heat, cook beef pieces until crispy.
  3. Remove beef pieces from pan and keep aside.
  4. Remove some of the oil from the pan and over medium heat, add onions, garlic, ginger and chillies.
  5. Once fragrant, add red pepper and stir fry.
  6. Add sauces: sriracha, ketchup, soy sauce and vinegar. Stir to combine and allow to come to a boil.
  7. When boiling, add back fried beef. Toss to coat and you're finished.
