Lasagna

Description

This quick and easy lasagna recipe is sure to bring smiles and rich aromas to any household. The layered dish is an Italian classic loved all over the world. This recipe is designed to be simple and tasty, serving 4-6 people. It has been passed down and adjusted to its current form by countless generations and kitchens.

We hope that you enjoy this recipe as much as possible. Have fun! Put your own twists and flavours on it!

Ingradients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Whatever else is lying around

Steps

  1. Chop 500g of tomatoes into quarters and spread them evenly in a baking tray.
  2. Add a light drizzle of olive oil as well as a sprinkle of salt and pepper over the tomatoes.
  3. Place the tray of the tomatoes in the oven at 180 degrees C for 15 minutes.
  4. Melt two tablespoons of butter in a small pot over low heat.
  5. Slowly alternate between adding flour and milk to the pot of melted butter while contantly stirring with a spatula.
  6. Once you have added a cup of milk in total, continue to add flour and stir until the consistency is thick but still liquid.
  7. From here on you are on your own. Remember to HAVE FUN.
